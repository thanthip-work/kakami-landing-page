Kakami

Customizable Beauty & Personal Care Products for Your Business

Purpose: This project will be a one-page website for an existing business: Kakami Wholesale & Private labeling. This page will be the "About Us" page for this business.

Audience: This site is designed with small business owners/entrepreneurs in mind. It is targeted to small businesses in beauty and personal care niche. Target audience to include: small business owners, store managers, or entrepreneurs that wants to save time and money sourcing ingredients from multiple vendors or handmade their own products.

Goal: To improve customer experience and to increase sales and conversion rates for an existing business: Kakami. By building a strong "About Us" page that focuses on highlighting the biggest selling points of the business.

Persona

Name: Mary Smith

Age: 32

Profession: Small business owner selling handmade products in her own brick and mortar store. She's looking to add more products to her store, but she does not have the manpower to handmade the products. She's looking to outsource this process to another company that can provide high-quality handmade products. She would love to be able to customize some aspects of the new products to her liking as well.

Technology: Window power user. Uses Dell - Inspiron 5410 2-in-1 14" touch-screen laptop with Firefox and Chrome with Android phone - Samsung Galaxy S22 with Google Chrome and Firefox.

Attitudes and Behaviors: short on time, running out of energy, and overwhelmed with running all the aspect of her business by herself. She wants all the information in front of her and the process to be straight forward without the hassle of calling or emailing for information or to order something.

Frustrations and Needs: Mary needs to outsource product making. She is looking for a company or someone that can provide high quality handmade items at a wholesale price. Ideally, she would be able to customize some aspect of the product to make the product unique to her brand.

Goals: Find a website that sell beautiful handmade products that accepts semi-customized orders. Does the website provides all the information she needs to know about the products? Mary doesn't want to waste time and wants the process to be easy and straight forward.

User Story: As a customer, I need to be able to order products for my business easily even customized ones.
