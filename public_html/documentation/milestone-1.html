Kakami

Customizable Beauty & Personal Care Products

Purpose

To improve Kakami's customer experience by bringing to life a feature that allows customers to easily semi-customize available items (i.e. color combination in a design of a product) and see the 2D mock-up of the customized product in real-time. As well as allowing customers to easily place their customized orders.

Audience

Small business owners in beauty and personal care product niche. Busy entrepreneurs that do not want to deal with sourcing ingredients from multiple vendors or make their own handmade products. As well as business owners who want to easily customize the look of the product/packaging to fit their brand.

Goal

To improve customer experience when ordering a customized product and to increase sales and conversion rates for an existing business: kakami.
