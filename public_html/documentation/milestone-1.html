Kakami

Customizable Beauty & Personal Care Products

Purpose: This project will be a landing page/one-page website that contains one-product and its 2D mock-up that allows customers to easily customize the colors of the product and see how the end product will look like in 2D in real-time.

Audience: This site is designed with small business owners/entrepreneurs in mind. It is targeted to small businesses in beauty and personal care niche. Target audience to include: small business owners, store managers, or entrepreneurs that wants to save time and money sourcing ingredients from multiple vendors or handmade their own products.

Goal: To improve customer experience when ordering a customized product and to increase sales and conversion rates for an existing business: Kakami. By bringing to life a feature that allows customers to easily semi-customize available items (i.e. color combination in a design of a product) and see the 2D mock-up of the customized product in real-time.
