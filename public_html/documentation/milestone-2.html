Kakami: User Experience and Interaction Design

Content Strategy

This site will be one page and will include the following sections:

Header: This is the top part of the website and will include Kakami logo.

Navigation: The navigation bar will be right below the header, the navigation bar will show the menu but no links will be attached to any of the options to keep this - a one-page website for the project. When the site is viewed on a mobile device, the navigation will turn into an icon with 3 stacked lines (aka a “hamburger”). Tapping the mobile navigation icon will trigger a vertical toggle menu. This section will also include a cart icon in the top right corner.

Top Section: The top section will feature an image that showcases the company's niche and gives the user a feel of what the brand is about.This top section will also include a short company slogan as well as a call-to-action button that says "Get Started".

How It Works: This section will briefly explain how product customization works on this website in 3 simple steps. This will also include simple images or illustrations to go along with the explanation of each step.

Who We Are & What We Do: This section will express the brand's unique identity - briefly telling its story. To introduce the brand and stating why it does what it does.In this section, negative space will be utilized in the layout design to give a clean, minimalist feel to the page.

Our Mission: This section contains a concise explanation of the business' purpose or its reason for existence aka a mission statement. The mission statement will be centered on the page in this section.

Gallery: This section consists of a few images of the different type of products available. This is aim to engage customers with more visual elements and give customers something new to discover i.e. a product category they might want to add to their business that they did not think of before. As well as to spark interest in new site visitors.

Contact Us: This is where customers go when they have a question or want to make an inquiry about their own project or product that they want to create. This section includes a simple "Contact Us" form where customers are required to input their name, email address, and message. There will also be a reCAPTCHA box to check and a submit button.

Footer: This section will be at the very bottom of the page and will include a social bar that contains social media icons that lead users to the business' social media pages. This footer will also contain menu to other pages a customer might look for - such as FAQs, Shipping Policy, etc. (no links to these pages will be attached at this time)
